Who We Are

Rooted in Nature,
Trusted Worldwide

We transform tropical harvests into high-quality, globally trusted ingredient solutions.

AthiTerra is a women-led food ingredient business specialised in premium gherkins. We bridge the gap between nature's finest produce and the world's demand for authentic, clean-label ingredients.

We work hand-in-hand with farming communities, maintaining rigorous quality standards and ethical sourcing practices that ensure every product carries the full integrity of its origin — from soil to shelf.

Our Team

Proudly Women-Led

AthiTerra is a women-led organisation where over 80% of our team members are women. From farm oversight and quality control to operations and client partnerships, women drive every stage of our business.

We believe that empowering women in agri-business creates stronger communities, more resilient supply chains, and better products. Our commitment to gender equity isn't just a policy — it's the foundation of who we are.
