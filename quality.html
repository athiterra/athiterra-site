Manufacturing Standards

Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP)

AthiTerra's processing operations follow Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) — the internationally recognised framework for ensuring that food products are consistently produced and controlled to quality standards appropriate for their intended use. Our GMP commitment covers every aspect of the production process:

🏭 Facility & Environment

Processing areas are designed for one-way product flow to prevent cross-contamination. Floors, walls, and equipment are made of food-safe, easy-to-clean materials. Temperature and humidity are monitored continuously.

🧼 Sanitation & Hygiene

Comprehensive cleaning and sanitation schedules are followed daily. All personnel follow strict hand-washing protocols, wear protective clothing, and pass through hygiene stations before entering production zones.

🧑‍🍳 Personnel Training

All production staff receive regular training on food safety, hygiene protocols, allergen management, and emergency procedures. Training records are documented and audited.

⚙️ Equipment & Maintenance

All processing equipment is food-grade, regularly calibrated, and maintained on a preventive schedule. Equipment logs track maintenance history and ensure operational readiness.

📝 Documentation & Records

Every production batch is documented with complete records — raw material receipts, processing parameters, quality test results, and packaging details. This enables full traceability and rapid issue resolution.

🐛 Pest Control & Water Safety

Integrated pest management programmes are in place with regular monitoring. Water used in processing meets potable water standards and is tested at defined intervals for microbiological and chemical compliance.
