Our Standards

Quality You Can Trust

At AthiTerra, quality is our culture. Every batch goes through a rigorous quality assurance process to meet international export standards consistently.

  • 01

    Farm-Level Inspection

    We partner directly with certified farms and conduct field inspections to ensure clean, pesticide-compliant produce from the source.

  • 02

    Grading & Sorting

    Automated and manual sorting ensures uniform size, colour, and texture eliminating defects before processing begins.

  • 03

    Hygienic Processing

    State-of-the-art facility maintaining strict hygiene protocols and full traceability throughout the production line.

  • 04

    Lab Testing & Certification

    Each batch is tested for microbiological safety, acidity levels, and shelf stability before release for export.
